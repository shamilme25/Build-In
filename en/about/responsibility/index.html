CORPORATE RESPONSIBILITY

The main asset of RD Construction is our team: up-and-coming young people staking on progress and final result as their career priorities. With regular attendance of educational programs in Europe and North America, the company’s specialists enjoy best conditions for work and professional growth, as they raise the level of their skills and gain valuable experience.

SAFETY. EFFECTIVENESS. RELIABILITY

Courtesy of the effective system of safety rules enforcement control, not a single failure or emergency have occurred on our construction sites during 18 years. RD Construction prioritizes the safe working environment for builders, contractors and employees. With each passing year we upgrade on-site control, bringing it to perfection. The accident prevention service performs the merit rating of construction sites and workplaces in terms of enforcement of fire and electric safety standards.

ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP

Caring for the environment is an essential condition of our company’s sustainable development. In our operations we try to minimize the negative impact of construction activities upon the environment, adhering to strict international environmental and sanitary standards as well as construction codes.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Being the leader of innovative construction, we think it our duty to boost the prestige of our profession and industry.
We daily demonstrate the ideal approach to construction activities by our own example, keeping up the apple-pie order on our sites and complying 100% with all safety requirements.

The high production culture and dead certainty that our construction sites are absolutely safe give us the nerve to make our projects under construction open for all visitors, including schoolchildren and orphanage alumni for whom we arrange educational tours.
