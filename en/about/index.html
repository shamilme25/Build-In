About Us

Build In is a leading developer of A-grade commercial, industrial and residential projects in Kerala.

Since its foundation the company has doubled its turnover year on year, with its staff numbers swelling accordingly. Today Build In has over 4,000 professionals on its payroll. The company is active in Kerala, CIS and Europe.
