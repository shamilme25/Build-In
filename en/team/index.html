Construction Department

The Construction Department consists of 6 highly qualified teams capable of building projects of any scale and level of complexity using international best practice and the most advanced technologies.

FIT-OUT Department

The department consists of project teams which implement some of the best commercial real estate fit-out projects across Russia.

QUALITY CONTROL AND WARRANTEES DEPARTMENT

This department controls the quality of materials and equipment as well as construction and installation work. Monitoring of the technical condition of construction sites and compliance with safety regulations is carried out on a continual basis.

ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

RD Construction has the strongest engineering team on the Russian construction market. This department is responsible for the technical aspects of project management: the development, review and issuance of project planning documentation as well as the implementation of effective engineering solutions.

Design Department

This department is engaged in the development of project design documentation for buildings.

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT department

This department is responsible for implementing the core strategy of the company and its development in new markets.

ADMINISTRATIVE DEPARTMENT

This department ensures the stable operation of all business divisions and branches of the company.
