RAIFFEISENBANK

The office of Raiffeisenbank was one of the largest projects on the Moscow office market. This project was completed in only 7 months, which considering the scale and technical complexity of the project is a very tight timeframe.

Project

RD Construction was responsible for the fit-out of the office premises, installation of engineering systems and modernization of core building systems.

    The implementation of such a large-scale project in just half a year is an ambitious goal which our team successfully achieved. We congratulate the employees of Raiffeisenbank on their move and wish them comfortable and productive work in their new office

    ANTON ZVANTSOV Fit-out Department Director

    • LAYOUT

      The business center, in addition to the open-space work zone, has training and educational classrooms, meeting rooms, conference halls as well as recreation areas, a doctor’s office, bank branch, sports hall, technical premises and server rooms.

      • TECHNOLOGIES

        A modern engineering system with a high degree of automation was installed in the building. In order to guarantee the uninterrupted functioning of all systems and maintain a comfortable climate in the office, an additional high-power cooler was installed on the roof of the business center.

        Particular attention had to be given to the creation of comfortable conditions for effective work, in part as a way to compensate employees for the insufficient transport accessibility of the building. Another important task was to resolve the issue of the lack of a sufficient number of places for the organization to holding business meetings and negotiations

        SVETLANA KORZHEVA EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR, RAIFFEISENBANK
