ALFA-LABORATORY

The office is built to accommodate 250 people and the work zone is organized in an open space layout. In addition to several isolated meeting rooms, the office has open offices and testing rooms, where new services are tried out. A transformer conference hall which can accommodated up to 50 people is situated the central part of the office.

Project

In the construction process much attention was paid to ensuring comfortable work conditions and facilitating communication among employees.

    After finishing work on the project, we can say that it turned out to be a really creative and functional office which resembles the headquarters of such American corporations as Google and Adobe. It was very pleasing to be able to work with a company that strives to attain the best world standards

    PAVEL KHACHATUROV Senior Project Manager

    • Construction

      Optimization of the project planning solutions along with a rational approach to the selection of materials and technologies made it possible to implement the project within a reasonable budget.

      • INTERIORS

        The main decorative theme for the interior is superheroes and street art. This concept is intended to emphasize the innovative aspects of the business and stimulate creativity among the laboratory’s specialists.

        The office interior is filled with bright colors and unusual geometric shapes. One of the unique aspects of the project is its colorful game zone, where employees can play ping pong or fussball. For work and leisure the office has an open-air terrace with designer furniture and special illuminators for after dark.

        It might seem that some enormous sum of money was invested in the innovative Alfa Laboratory. However, the set-up of this space cost less than a standard office which would not be worth mentioning in the ‘Interior of the Week’ column

        ALEXANDER ANIKIN Project MANAGER, ALFA-BANK
