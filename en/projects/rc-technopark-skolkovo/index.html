Housing project Technopark in Skolkovo

Residential quarters lie within the territory of Innovation Center Skolkovo and represent a cluster of mid-rise houses with apartments for teachers and students as well as social infrastructure.

Project

There is a tight construction deadline. Daily engaged at the site are 1,250 workers with 60 engineers supervising the construction. The company set to the works in March 2015, while the termination of construction is scheduled for the second quarter of 2016.

    The architectural concept

    The architectural concept was developed by the French studio Agence d`Architecture A. Bech, UNK Project, BRT RUS.

    Harmonious blending of the urban environment with natural landscape underlies the concept.

    In each of the three quarters construction is led by experienced project managers having several hi-tech and large-scale residential and industrial real estate projects behind their shoulders. Overall more than 1,000 specialists will be involved

    ILYA BAYBUS Business Development Director
