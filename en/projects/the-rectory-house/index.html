REKTORSKY DOM

Rektorsky Dom is a three-story historical mansion with offices organized in the standard office-block layout.

The building is equipped with all the necessary building support systems, video security and access control and also has 24-hour security for the property’s territory and parking.

    Construction

    RD Construction completely refurbished the building: architecture of the 18th-century facades and unique finishing elements were renovated. When RD Construction started renovation work on Rektorsky Dom, the building was almost completely destroyed. Each brick was carefully restored and replaced into the walls of the building as an element of the unique finishing.

    HISTORY

    Rektorsky Dom is a private residence built in 1740s in the very heart of Moscow, a former palace of the noble Volkonsky family. At the end of the 18th century, the building included the apartments for the rector and professors of Moscow State University.

    RD Construction is very respectful of the architectural heritage of historic buildings, so the renovation work was performed in strict observance of the appropriate precautions and with the use of state-of-the-art engineering technologies
