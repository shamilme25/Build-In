Project

The given project is one of the most ambitious and large-scale on the office real estate market.

    The headquarters is built in line with the highest quality standards, boasting up-to-date utilities and all engineering equipment controlled from a single center using an intelligent automated control system. Used in the fit-out of premises were environmentally benign materials whose high quality is corroborated by certificates of conformity.

    This project showcased the professionalism of RD Construction’s team and the high quality of works, as we brought to fruition, on a tight schedule, one of the most ambitious projects on the Russian fit-out market

    ANTON ZVANTSOV Fit-out Department Director

    • Layout

      The ground floor of the business center features reception, lounge, conference hall seating 200 guests; found on floors two to six is open space working area. Floor seven will accommodate top management offices, board of directors meeting room and talk rooms.

      • Opening

        The opening ceremony was attended by Vice Governor of St. Petersburg Mikhail Mokretsov, Deputy Chairman of Management Committee Gazprom Alexander Medvedev, CEO LLC Gazprom Export Elena Burmistrova, representatives of the city administration.

        We are opening a brand new office of Gazprom Export in “Russia’s most Europeanized city”, which is symbolic, given that Europe will be in ever greater need of the Russian gas. Saint Petersburg is becoming an international center of natural gas trade, with already a fourth of all Gazprom administration employees working there

        Алексей Миллер
