This project was implemented in 8 months, which is a record time for projects of this scale and level of technology.

    We were given the task of creating a technically complex building with modern engineering systems and at the same time a comfortable working office for 2500 employees.     • International standards

      Alfa-Bank’s call center is equipped according to world-class standards for the organization of the work process at contact centers.

      During construction special attention was given to ensuring the requirements for self-sufficiency, uninterrupted service and automation as well as workplace ergonomics. The most advanced engineering infrastructure was installed in the building, including climate control and fire prevention and warning systems.
