Project

Zagorodny Kvartal is a low-rise residential complex with well-developed infrastructure located in the municipal district of Khimki. The general concept for the development was created by one of the leading urban development firms – the California-based Calthorpe Associates, whose architects specialize in comprehensive territory development and which has implemented a number of major projects in the United States, Europe and Asia.

In June 2014 the complex was a winner of the Real Estate Records Awards in the nomination for Best Low-Rise Complex.

    TECHNOLOGIES

    In the construction of the apartment buildings and townhouses a ventilated façade system and polyurethane foam roofing materials are being used which allows for heat retention while also preventing the accumulation of moisture or overheating of the premises during hot weather. These technologies along with efficient engineering equipment not only maintain a pleasant climate inside the buildings in all weather conditions but all provide substantial savings on energy resources.

    Today work is proceeding strictly according to schedule and in compliance with the agreed requirements. Everyday approximately 200 builders are working on site under the supervision of the top specialists of the company

    VLADIMIR SCHAPOV Senior Project Manager

    • ENGINEERING

      The underground portion of the buildings is used for a parking facility, which meets the most advanced quality requirements and corresponds to international standards.

      Since the operation of the engineering systems in the underground portion of the buildings creates additional noise, the specialists of RD Construction undertook a number of soundproofing measures to guarantee the highest level of comfort for the building’s residents.
