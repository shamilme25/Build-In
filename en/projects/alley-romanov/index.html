Romanov Alley

Moscow’s first pedestrian gastronomic street Romanov Alley.

ADDRESS
Moscow, between Mokhovaya Street and Romanov Pereulok
TOTAL AREA
7 000 m²
Year
2016
role
General contractor

Project

The ground-level premises along Romanov Alley will be home to quaint cafes and restaurants, gastronomic shops and boutiques, as well as a culinary school. Business-class apartments with hotel service will be situated in the upper levels of the buildings along the street.

    The project included the restoration of buildings erected in the 1890s. The property’s location in the center of the city and close to residential and office buildings created some additional requirements which impacted the entire work process

    NIKOLAI MAKARENKO Project Manager

    • TECHNOLOGIES

      The street is paved with heated stone blocks which are decorated with backlit inscriptions of pleasant salutations in four world languages

      The project includes the restoration of historical buildings dating back to the end of the 18th century. The first phase of Romanov Alley was completed in October 2012, and the entire construction project is scheduled for completion in 2016.
