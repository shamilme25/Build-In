This office, one of the ten largest headquarters in Moscow, includes a back office, bank branch, data center and a hi-tech trading hall capable of accommodating 150 traders (one of the largest trading halls in Russia).

    In this project it was important for us to achieve the right balance between the technological component and the image component. The large trading hall which was incorporated into the office space deserves particular attention, as the client had very high expectations for this space. The headquarters of the financial corporation has taken a prominent place not only in our portfolio but also among similar offices in Russia
