BEELINE DATA CENTER

The data center consists of a group of buildings on a territory of 30,000 m².

Project

In addition to the main building, the campus of the data center includes substations and energy modules for diesel rotary uninterruptible power supply devices (DRUPS), a boiler room, fuel storage, waste treatment and various auxiliary structures.

    In 2014 the data center received Tier III reliability certification according to the classifications of the Uptime Institute.
