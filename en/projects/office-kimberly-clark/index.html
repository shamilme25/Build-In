Project

The new office is split into two functional zones: a work area and a recreation zone with a cafeteria. The office has an open space layout. In addition to the ordinary meeting rooms, the office has compact sound-proof rooms for confidential telephone conversations.

    In working on the new office we took into consideration all of the requirements of Kimberly-Clark. In the process, the plans were adapted to create the most functional and comfortable work space possible. Throughout all stages from project planning to completion of construction, we worked in close cooperation with representatives of the client, which played an important role in the successful implementation of this project

    VALERY PROKOFIEV Deputy Technical Director

    • INTERIOR

      One of the most successful designer solutions was the use of unifying concept for the meeting rooms through a common design theme: each of the meeting rooms is named after one of the planets of our solar system and is decorated with the relevant cosmic images.

      Our company’s move to the new office is another step toward the formation of a special atmosphere based on the principles of trust, cooperation and creativity. The new office reflects Kimberly-Clark’s ambition to remain the best place to work for our employees and to fully correspond to the high corporate standards which the company adheres to around the world

      JULIA CHIRKOVA CORPORATE SERVICES & COMMUNICATIONS MANAGER FOR EASTERN EUROPE, KIMBERLY-CLARK
