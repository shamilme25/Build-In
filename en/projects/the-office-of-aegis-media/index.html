Aegis media

The office of Aegis Media is located on three floors of the Imperia Tower at Moscow City. One of the aims of the project was to bring together under one roof six agencies of the group which previously had been located in different parts of the city.

Project

This office includes comfortable work zones and common areas which offer an alternative work space for individuals or meetings of project teams. All meeting rooms are equipped with Creston smart building systems, which manage lighting, blinds and multimedia equipment as well as centralized air conditioning.

    In the creation of the office for the creative team of the communications group we used interesting visual effects.
