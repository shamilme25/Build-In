Squire Patton Boggs

The office of the international law firm is very pragmatic, setting off the firm’s business status and reputation and combining comfort for the employees with confiding atmosphere for communicating with clients.

ADDRESS
Moscow
TOTAL AREA
1 000 m²
YEAR
2015
ROLE
General contractor
Customer

Project

The facility features a reception zone, four meeting rooms, 25 walled offices, open space as well as premises for an archive, servers, in addition to a couple of coffee points. The Russian division of Squire Patton Boggs has 50 personnel.

    DESIGN

    The office concept is pivoted on the low-key style – the reception zone is designed in corporate colors – as well as confiding atmosphere for clients. Given the high status and business reputation of the client, most cutting-edge materials and technological solutions were used in the project.

    In the course of the project management the team used RD Construction’s standards: painstaking initial elaboration on the budget, rigorous selection of contractors, direct negotiations with suppliers and settlements in rubles where possible. All of these enabled the team to complete the project within the set 3.5 months without any crash effort or rush job.

    ALEXANDER STYSHNEV Senior Project Manager

    • Office Solutions

      One of the eye-catching solutions is the floor-to-ceiling glazing of top management’s offices overlooking the sunny side; hence the low-emission coatings inherent in the design. The engineering team installed powerful and noiseless convectors and fan coils.

      ENERGY EFFICIENCY

      The lighting design is maximally energy efficient – solely LED lights matching the “green” BREEAM standards were used to claim respective certification of the office building.

      As we were selecting the general contractor, we looked for a company having rich experience in delivering successful office projects for international clients; this is why we stayed our choice on RD Construction. The elaborate project and its quality implementation made our new office convenient both for clients and employees of the firm.

      Yulia Anisimova Director of Administration and Finance, Moscow Office of Squire Patton Boggs
